Hassle Free Shopping

Dropshipping is a business model primarily used by e-commerce retailers that do not stock the inventory themselves. It is a fulfillment method where a retailer receives orders for products listed on the shop front, which are then forwarded to the supplier for shipping. This is a win-win situation for both retailers and suppliers as they can focus on just the business process they are expert in—retailers can nurture customer relationships and suppliers maintai inventory and ship products.
